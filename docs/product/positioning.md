# Condo Charge Product Positioning

## Core Statement

Condo Charge is not an app to simply check whether a charger is available.

Condo Charge is a smart platform for condominium charging management.

## What Condo Charge Is

- A product that connects infrastructure, residents, and administrators
- A control layer for shared EV charging operations
- A communication and monitoring platform for condominium charging service
- A pilot-ready solution designed for real operational visibility

## What Condo Charge Is Not

- Not just a charger-status viewer
- Not a generic EV app
- Not only a technical integration around Legrand hardware

## Positioning For Administrators

Condo Charge helps the condominium administrator present charging as an organized, transparent, and monitorable service.

## Positioning Keywords

- smart
- condominium-first
- operational
- monitorable
- premium
- mobile-first
